SubStar helps youth soccer coaches track playtime, suggest optimal substitutions, and manage player rotations—effortlessly and fairly.

Get the App

Features

Track Player Time

Monitor playtime in seconds or by substitutions to ensure fair rotations across the team.

Drag-and-Drop Interface

Easily manage player positions with intuitive touch-and-drag functionality.

Goalie and Field Logic

Configure lineups with or without goalies, depending on your game format.

Smart Sub Suggestions

Get substitution suggestions to balance time and effort for every player.

Customizable Settings

Change player counts, timing methods, and goalie options to fit your team’s needs.

Offline Functionality

No internet? No problem. SubStar works completely offline on your device.
